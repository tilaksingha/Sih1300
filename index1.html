Problem Statement Title Automated Public Lighting


Description

Public lighting in roads and public areas are lit even during daytime resulting in the wastage of precious energy. The conventional method being employed is manual switching on and off and in some places, there has been a shift towards timer control for the on-off of public lighting. However, this has not been very effective. System to be simulated by the participants Desired Outcome: Smart public lighting systems, that are centrally controlled by IOT would facilitate dynamical adjustment of illumination and provide a record of the consumption. This would dramatically result in lower operating costs and would aid in low downtime of failed lighting systems as the defective locations can be identified.

Idea/Approach Details

  • It is an IOT based project on “Automated Public Lighting” which aims to create an intelligent ,energy & cost efficient smart public lighting system for public areas, corporate sectors, parking lots and so on.
  • The system will automatically adjust the brightness of public lights based on atmospheric light intensity and the presence of pedestrians or vehicles.
  • This system can also efficiently display its power consumption on google sheets and is more cost effective than other existing public light systems.

Technology Stack

Our Technology stack :

  • Arduino UNO
  • ESP32
  • IR Proximity Sensor
  • LDR Sensor (ANG & DIG)
  • Jumper Wires
  • PZEM004T with CC

Use Cases

  • Street Lighting:
    • Efficiently illuminates streets and pathways for enhanced safety with reduced energy costs in villages and rural areas.
    • Improves urban streetscape while conserving energy in cities and urban areas.
  • Pedestrian Walkways: Illuminates sidewalks and crosswalks for pedestrian safety.
  • Parking Lots: Ensures well-lit and secure parking facilities.
  • Public Spaces: Lights up parks, community centers, and gathering areas for residents.
  • Public Transportation: Illuminates bus stops and transit hubs for passenger safety.
  • Other uses and implementations can also be done with this system.

Dependencies

  • Stable Power Supply: Uninterrupted power is essential for continuous system operation.
  • Sensor Accuracy: The reliability of the system depends on accurate IR sensor readings.
  • Google Sheets Integration: Accurate data collection relies on Google Sheets functionality.
  • Maintenance: Regular maintenance and updates to keep the system running smoothly.

Show Stoppers

  • Power source instability.
  • Faulty LED lights.
